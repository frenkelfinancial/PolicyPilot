AI Underwriter

One of the biggest pain points in life insurance sales is not knowing which carrier will actually approve your client. Age, health conditions, medications, build — every carrier has different guidelines, and guessing costs you apps and commission.

The AI Underwriter solves this with live data from every major carrier. Enter your client's health profile and instantly see which carriers are likely to approve, at which rate class — before you ever submit an application.

  • Live carrier approval likelihood by health profile
  • Rate class predictions (Preferred, Standard, Substandard)
  • Side-by-side carrier comparison before you apply
  • Stop wasting apps on carriers that won't approve
