How do I get started?
Click "Get Started" in the nav, create your account, and import your leads via CSV or paste them in directly. You can be calling within minutes. We're also happy to walk you through setup over the phone — just text us.
What do I need to use the Power Dialer?
Just a phone — any phone. You call into a host number, enter your PIN, and Producer Stack automatically connects you to the next lead the moment you hang up. No app, no headset, no VoIP setup required.
Can I cancel any time?
Yes, absolutely. There are no long-term contracts or cancellation fees. You can cancel from your account settings at any time and your subscription simply won't renew.
How long does setup take?
Most producers are fully set up in under 10 minutes. Import your leads, connect your carrier credentials for rate quotes, and you're ready to go. If you hit any snags, call or text us and we'll get you sorted fast.
Can I switch plans later?
Yes. You can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time from your account settings. Upgrades take effect immediately; downgrades apply at the next billing cycle.
Is there a free trial?
Yes — click "Start Free Trial" to get access with no credit card required. Explore the platform and call us if you have questions during your trial.
