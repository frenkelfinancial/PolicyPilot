Underwriting Term Guide
LEVEL / SELECT 1 / PREFERRED
Best health tier. Day 1 full coverage. Full commission.
SELECT 2 / STANDARD
Moderate health issues, slightly higher premium. Day 1 coverage. Full commission.
GRADED
Limited death benefit first 2 yrs (Yr1 <30%, Yr2 <70%, Yr3 = 100%). ~40% commission cut.
GI / ROP (Return of Premium)
2-year waiting period. Death in Yr1-2 returns premiums only. Full benefit after 2 years. ~40% commission cut.
