Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 3, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Frenkel Financial LLC, a Wisconsin limited liability company, doing business as Producer Stack ("Producer Stack," "we," "us," or "our"), collects, uses, discloses, and safeguards information in connection with the Producer Stack CRM web and mobile application (the "App"), a customer relationship management, dialing, and quoting tool for licensed life insurance producers. This Policy should be read together with our Terms of Service, which governs your use of the App. By creating an account or using the App, you agree to the practices described in this Policy.

1. Information We Collect

a. Account & Profile Information. When you register, we collect your name, email address, password (stored in hashed form by our authentication provider and never visible to us in plain text), phone number, National Producer Number (NPN), the states in which you are licensed, and, if you enroll under an upline or team leader, the agency/enrollment code linking your account to that hierarchy.

b. Client & Lead Information You Enter. The App allows you to store information about your own insurance prospects and clients ("Client Data"), which may include names, contact details, dates of birth, addresses, health information relevant to insurance underwriting, income and financial information, and policy details. As between you and Producer Stack, you are the controller of Client Data. You are solely responsible for ensuring you have the legal right and any required consent to collect, store, and process this information, and for complying with applicable law, including state insurance privacy regulations and the Gramm-Leach-Bliley Act (GLBA), with respect to your clients' nonpublic personal information. We act as your service provider (processor) with respect to Client Data and process it only as necessary to provide, secure, and support the App — not for our own independent purposes.

Sensitive information. Health information relevant to underwriting is sensitive personal information under laws such as the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA/CPRA). We do not use health information you enter for any purpose other than powering the App's quoting and underwriting-estimate features, and we do not sell it, share it for advertising, or use it to build profiles about your clients.

c. Commission, Production & Team Data. The App allows you to record commission levels and percentages, policies submitted and issued, production totals and progress toward carrier bonus tiers, and — if you belong to an agency or downline — production visibility shared with your upline/team leader as configured within the App.

d. Calling & Communications Data. If you use the App's calling features, our telephony service providers process the phone numbers you dial, call duration, call status/outcome, and — if you grant microphone permission — audio needed to place and connect the call. Depending on your subscription plan and the features you enable, calls placed through the App may be recorded and retained for a plan-dependent period. See Section 3 of our Terms of Service for your obligations regarding call recording consent and telemarketing law.

e. Device, Usage & Diagnostic Information. We may automatically collect information about the device and app version you use, your operating system, IP address, and general usage or diagnostic data to operate, secure, and improve the App. We do not use third-party advertising trackers or analytics pixels (e.g., ad-network or social-media pixels) in the App.

f. Locally Stored Data. Some data (such as policy records, leads, quotes, and personal settings) is stored locally on your device. Clearing your browser/app storage may permanently delete this information unless it has also been saved to our servers.

2. How We Use Information

  • To provide, operate, and maintain the App
  • To authenticate you and keep your account secure
  • To power quoting, AI-assisted underwriting estimate tools, lead and policy tracking, commission tracking, and calling features
  • To send account-related communications, including an optional daily activity digest
  • To detect, prevent, and address technical issues, fraud, or misuse
  • To comply with legal obligations

We do not sell your personal information, and we do not use Client Data for advertising purposes.

Underwriting estimates are not guarantees. The App's AI-assisted and rule-based underwriting tools produce a general estimate of likely carrier classification based on the information entered. They are not an application, quote, or binding offer, and actual underwriting decisions are made solely by the insurance carrier. See our Terms of Service for the full disclaimer.

3. How Information Is Shared

We share information with service providers ("subprocessors") that help us operate the App, each of which may access information only as needed to provide their service to us, and each of which is contractually bound to protect information with confidentiality and data-protection obligations consistent with this Policy:

  • Supabase — database hosting, authentication, and backend functions
  • SignalWire / Telnyx — telephony, including placing and (where enabled) recording calls, and provisioning phone numbers
  • DeepSeek — AI-assisted parsing of health information you submit, used solely to generate underwriting-condition estimates within the App
  • Stripe — subscription billing and payment processing
  • Insurance carrier portals & quoting tools (e.g., ITK) — third-party quoting and product data accessed from within the App

We may also disclose information if required by law or legal process, to protect the rights, property, or safety of Producer Stack, our users, or others, or in connection with a merger, acquisition, financing, or sale of assets, with notice to affected users where required by law.

4. Telemarketing, Do-Not-Call & Call Recording

The App provides dialing infrastructure that you use to contact your own leads and clients. You are solely responsible for complying with the Telephone Consumer Protection Act (TCPA), state and federal Do-Not-Call regulations, permitted calling hours, and obtaining any consent required before contacting a person. Producer Stack does not verify that numbers you upload or dial have been scrubbed against Do-Not-Call registries or that you have consent to call them.

Where call recording is enabled for your plan, you are solely responsible for complying with all applicable call-recording and wiretapping laws, including obtaining any required notice or consent from call participants in two-party/all-party consent states. Full responsibility-shifting terms are set out in our Terms of Service.

5. Data Security

We use administrative, technical, and physical safeguards designed to protect information from unauthorized access, use, alteration, or disclosure, including encryption of data in transit (HTTPS/TLS) and encryption of data at rest (AES-256, provided by our database infrastructure). We maintain access controls limiting who can reach production data. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. Data Breach Notification

If we experience a breach of security compromising personal information, we will notify affected users and any required regulators as required by applicable law, including Wis. Stat. § 134.98 and other applicable state breach-notification statutes.

7. Data Retention

We retain account information for as long as your account remains active. Client Data is retained until you delete it or close your account, after which it will be deleted or de-identified within a commercially reasonable time, except where retention is required by law. Call recordings, where applicable, are retained for the period specified by your subscription plan.

You can review, update, or correct most account information directly within the App's Settings. Depending on your location, you may also have the right to request access to, correction of, export of, or deletion of personal information we hold about you by contacting us using the information below.

California Residents. Under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA/CPRA), California residents have the right to know what personal information is collected, to request deletion of that information, and to opt out of the sale or sharing of personal information. We do not sell or share personal information as those terms are defined under the CCPA, and we do not use any analytics or advertising technology that would constitute "sharing."

EEA / UK Residents. If the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) or UK GDPR applies to you, you may have additional rights, including the right to access, rectify, erase, restrict, or port your data, and to object to certain processing. To exercise these rights, contact us using the information below.
